CYBER THREAT LIBRARY
Understand the digital threats our AI is guarding you against.
SQL Injection (SQLi)
CRITICAL RISK
A nightmare scenario where hackers "talk" directly to your database. They inject malicious commands into your website forms to trick the database into handing over private data.
Danger Level
Can lead to total data theft of all user emails, passwords, and sensitive records.
AI Response
Instant IP Quarantine and Payload Scrubbing.
DDoS / Denial of Service
CRITICAL RISK
Imagine a million people trying to enter your shop at the same second. Your server can't handle the load and crashes, making your website unreachable for real customers.
Danger Level
Causes total business shutdown and loss of revenue while the attack lasts.
AI Response
Dynamic Traffic Filtering and Rate-Limiting.
Botnet Activity
CRITICAL RISK
Networks of thousands of "zombie" infected computers (or smart-fridges) controlled by a single hacker to perform automated attacks against your site.
Danger Level
Highly automated and relentless. Can be used for massive spam or coordinated hacking.
AI Response
Pattern-based Cluster Blocking.
Brute Force / Guessing
HIGH RISK
Hackers use "robots" to try thousands of password combinations a second on your login page until they kick the door down and get inside.
Danger Level
Leads to account takeovers and unauthorized access to your private dashboard.
AI Response
Auto-Lockdown after detection of repeated failures.
Cross-Site Scripting (XSS)
HIGH RISK
A sneaky attack where hackers inject malicious code into the pages your users see. The code can steal their login tokens or "impersonate" your website.
Danger Level
Undermines your users' trust and can lead to their personal accounts being stolen.
AI Response
JavaScript Sandbox Isolation & Block.
Port Scanning
MEDIUM RISK
Digital "window peeping." Hackers scan your server to see which "doors" (ports) are open or unlocked as a first step to launch a bigger attack.
Danger Level
It is the reconnaissance phase. It means someone is specifically looking for a way in.
AI Response
Host Cloaking and Recon Detection.
